## Wrap Lines

Use hotkey ```Alt+Z``` or SETTING: ```Ctrl+,``` then search for ```word wrap``` keyword

## Collapse code ranges

```Ctrl+K Ctrl+<number>``` for example ```Ctrl+K Ctrl+3``` 

⚠️Note: In [Cursor](https://www.cursor.com/): ```Ctrl+M Ctrl+3```
